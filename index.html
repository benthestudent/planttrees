Our Earth is hurting...

but YOU can help.

Humans cut down 15 billion trees every year. Help us replant them.

By watering the tree and answering trivia questions, you can help us generate revenue to be donated to the following non-profits, devoted to reversing the detrimental effects of deforestation and climate change.

Hommes Et Terre, a landscape restoration and agroforestry company that plants trees across Burkina Faso, slowly reforesting the Sahel deserts.

Fundacion DIA, an organization based in Nicaragua, Goldstandard certified in 2014, which is preserving the remaining Nicaraguan rainforest.

Eden Reforestation Projects, an organization which works to bring trees and vegetation to various locations worldwide, including Nepal, Madagascar, Haiti, Indonesia, Kenya and Mozambique, by helping local villages plant trees.

Foundation Green Ethiopia, an organization which aims to reforest Ethiopia with greenery and trees

Masarang Foundation, based in Indonesia and created to reforest the Masarang Mountain, this project that began in 2000 and has since expanded immensly, solving problems worldwide, such as deforestation, biodiversity loss, climate change, poverty and underdevelopment.
